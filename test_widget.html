Welcome to TechnoDexterous

This is a sample host website representing a page on technodexterous.com.

If the Flask API is running, you should see the chatbot widget floating on the bottom right of the screen.

Testing the Implementation:

  • Click the blue widget icon on the bottom right to open the chat.
  • Courses: Ask "What courses do you have?" or "How do I enroll?"
  • Getting Started: Ask "How do I sign up?"
  • Safety Rule: Ask "Delete my account" or "Change my password".
  • Smart Fallback: Type gibberish to see the suggestion buttons render!
